Remote & Scalable 3D Printing

Our Vision 🕶

Our goal is optimize print density on the printbed. We noticed most of the printbed
is not utilized during a single print. And, it also doesn't make sense to fly passengers
one by one in airplanes. Tertium's platform will allow multiple users to get
a "seat" on a printbed before takeoff ... remotley.

What we're building

An online platform that allows owners of 3D printers to allow creaters to upload parts to be printed on their device

How we're building it

Our current focus is developeing the site in parallel with a HW solution that can allow any printer to connect to our network

Why we're building it

3D Printers will be IOT devices. People want access remotley. And from the C19 pandemic, we NEED to connect makers to resources
