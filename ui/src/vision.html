Our story

Our service was created out of the COVID-19 crisis. As medical personal ran out of personal protection equipment (PPE), some tech-savvy doctors and nurses designed their own. However, most of them did not have access to 3D printers. Our team began to connect with those medical personnel and hospitals and began to 3D print, not just PPE, but also adapters for legacy equipment. As trusted by the healthcare industry, you will be able to trust us to deliver you the best quality prints.
