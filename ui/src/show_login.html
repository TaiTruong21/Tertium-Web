Online 3D Printing Service

Who are we?

TERTIUM is an industry-leading online 3D printing platform.
By using our state-of-the-art cloud printing platform, you would be connected to a network of designers, 3d printers, and makers.

No 3D Printer? No Problem

With TERTIUM, you would be able to submit your CAD or STL files into our database.
Nearby makers with 3D printers would then print out your file - and depending on how close you are, you could pick it up or get it delivered same day.

Have a 3D Printer? Make some Cash

With TERTIUM and your 3D printer, you would be connected to the network as maker.
Not only would you be helpung make drawings come to life, you would also be making some cash on the side.
